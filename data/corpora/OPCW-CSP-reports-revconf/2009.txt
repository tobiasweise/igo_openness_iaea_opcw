2. AGENDA ITEM TWO  Election of the Chairperson 
 
In accordance with Rules 34 and 35 of its Rules of Procedure, the Conference, by acclamation, elected as its Chairperson Ambassador Vaidotas Verba of Lithuania, who shall hold office until his successor is elected at the next regular session of the Conference. 
 
3. AGENDA ITEM THREE  Election of Vice-Chairpersons and other officers

3.2 Also in accordance with Rules 34 and 35 of its Rules of Procedure, the Conference elected, as Chairperson of the Committee of the Whole, Ambassador Aizaz Ahmad Chaudhry of Pakistan, who shall hold office until a new Chairperson is elected at the next regular session of the Conference.

9. 
 AGENDA ITEM NINE  Status of implementation of the Convention 



 
Subitem 9(a): Progress report on the implementation of the plan of action regarding the implementation of Article VII obligations 
9.1
 The Conference at its Thirteenth Session adopted a decision regarding the implementation of Article VII obligations (C-13/DEC.7, dated 5 December 2008), in which it requested the Technical Secretariat (hereinafter the Secretariat), inter alia, to provide its annual report on the status of implementation of Article VII obligations to the Executive Council (hereinafter the Council) at its Fifty-Eighth Session.  In its decision, the Conference also requested the Council to submit the report to it at its Fourteenth Session, along with its recommendations, as appropriate, for consideration. 



 
9.2
 The Conference considered and noted a report on the status of implementation of Article VII of the Chemical Weapons Convention (hereinafter the Convention) as at 19 August 2009 (C-14/DG.9, dated 21 October 2009). 



 
9.3
 The Conference also adopted a decision on the matter (C-14/DEC.12, dated 4 December 2009). 



 
Subitem 9(b): Implementation of the regime governing the handling of confidential information by the Technical Secretariat in 2008 
9.4
 The Conference considered and noted a report by the Director-General on the implementation of the regime governing the handling of confidential information by the Secretariat in 2008 (EC-55/DG.6 C-14/DG.1, dated 27 January 2009). 



 
Subitem 9(c): Progress made in meeting revised deadlines for the destruction of chemical weapons 
9.5
 Pursuant to a decision by the Conference at its Eleventh Session (paragraph 9.12(b) of C-11/5, dated 8 December 2006), the Conference considered and noted a report by the Director-General on the progress made by those States Parties that have been granted extensions of deadlines for the destruction of their Category 1 chemical weapons (C-14/DG.11, dated 19 November 2009).  



 
9.6
 Pursuant to the decisions by the Conference at its Eleventh Session on the final extended destruction deadline, the Conference noted with concern that the final extended destruction deadline of 29 April 2012 may not be fully met.  While noting that significant progress has been made, the Conference also noted that over 48% of chemical weapons stockpiles remain to be destroyed as of 1 December 2009.  In this regard, the Conference urged all possessor States Parties to take every necessary measure with a view to ensuring their compliance with the final extended destruction deadline. 



 
9.7
 The Conference welcomed the request by the Council at its Fifty-Eighth Session for its Chairperson to engage in informal consultations on how and when to initiate discussion by the Council on issues related to meeting the final extended deadlines for the destruction of chemical weapons.   



 
 Subitem 9(d): Extension of deadlines for the destruction of Category 1 chemical weapons stockpiles 
9.8
 The Conference considered and adopted a decision on the extension of intermediate and final deadlines for the destruction by the Libyan Arab Jamahiriya of its Category 1 chemical weapons (C-14/DEC.3, dated 2 December 2009). 



 
 Subitem 9(e): Implementation of Article X 
9.9
 The Conference at its Twelfth Session requested the Council to conduct intensive deliberations to develop measures for emergency assistance to Member States, including with regard to the victims of chemical weapons, as provided in Article X  of the Convention, and to report to the Conference at its Thirteenth Session,  along with any appropriate recommendations (paragraph 21.8 of C-12/7, dated 9 November 2007). 



 
9.10
 The Conference at its Thirteenth Session requested the Council to continue further intensive deliberations in line with the above-mentioned mandate given to it by the Conference at its Twelfth Session, and to report to the Conference at its Fourteenth Session (paragraph 9.9 of C-13/5, dated 5 December 2008). 



 
9.11
 Pursuant to the recommendation of the Council at its Fifty-Eighth Session (paragraph 5.27 of EC-58/9, dated 16 October 2009), the Conference requested the Council to continue to deliberate with a view to developing further measures for emergency assistance to Member States, including with regard to the victims of chemical weapons, as provided in Article X of the Convention, and to report back to the Conference at its Fifteenth Session. 

 Subitem 9(f): Guidelines regarding low-concentration limits for declarations of Schedule 2A and 2A* chemicals 


9.12 The Conference considered and adopted a decision on the guidelines regarding low-concentration limits for declarations of Schedule 2A and 2A* chemicals (C-14/DEC.4, dated 2 December 2009).

13. AGENDA ITEM THIRTEEN  Programme and Budget of the OPCW for 2010, submitted by the Executive Council, and all items pertaining to this Budget 
 
13.1 In accordance with subparagraph 21(a) of Article VIII of the Convention, and pursuant to Financial Regulation 3.6(a), the Conference considered and adopted the Programme and Budget of the OPCW for 2010, which the Council had submitted to it (C-14/DEC.8, dated 2 December 2009). 
13.2 The Medium-Term Plan for the period from 2010 to 2012 (EC-57/S/8 C-14/S/1, dated 10 July 2009 and Corr.1, dated 25 November 2009) was circulated to the Member States, along with the Draft Programme and Budget for 2010. 
14. AGENDA ITEM FOURTEEN  Scale of assessments to be paid by States Parties 
 In accordance with paragraph 21(b) of Article VIII of the Convention, the Conference adopted the scale of assessments to be paid by States Parties for the financial year 2010 (C-14/DEC.10, dated 4 December 2009).  
15. AGENDA ITEM FIFTEEN  External Auditors report on the audited financial statements of the OPCW for 2008 
 
 The Conference noted the financial statements of the OPCW and the report of the External Auditor for the year ending 31 December 2008 (EC-57/DG.13* C-14/DG.4, dated 24 June 2009 and Corr.1, dated 15 July 2009) which was forwarded to it by the Council in accordance with Regulation 13.10 of the OPCW Financial Regulations and Rules. 
 
16. AGENDA ITEM SIXTEEN  Administrative and financial matters 
 
Subitem 16(a): OPCW income and expenditure for the financial year to 30 June 2009; and the cash situation and the use of the Working Capital Fund for the financial year to 31 August 2009 
16.1
 The Council at its Fifty-Eighth Session considered and forwarded to the Conference a report by the Director-General on OPCW income and expenditure for the financial year to 30 June 2009 (EC-58/DG.2, dated 7 August 2009 and Corr.1, dated 8 September 2009).  The Conference noted this report. 



 
16.2
 Further to a decision by the Conference at its Thirteenth Session (paragraph 14 of C-13/DEC.5, dated 5 December 2008), the Conference noted a Note by the Director-General on the cash situation and the use of the Working Capital Fund  for the financial year to 31 August 2009 (EC-58/DG.6 C-14/DG.6, dated 28 September 2009). 



 
Subitem 16(b):  Status of implementation by States Parties of agreed multi-year payment plans to regularise the payment of their outstanding annual contributions 
16.3
 Pursuant to a decision by the Conference at its Eleventh Session (C-11/DEC.5, dated 7 December 2006), the Conference considered and noted the Note by the Director-General on status of implementation by States Parties of agreed multi-year payment plans (EC-58/DG.7 C-14/DG.7, dated 28 September 2009). 



 
Subitem 16(c):  Report of the Office of Internal Oversight for 2008 
16.4
 In accordance with Regulation 12.5 of the OPCW Financial Regulations and Rules, the Council at its Fifty-Sixth Session considered and forwarded to the Conference the annual report of the Office of Internal Oversight for the period from 1 January to 31 December 2008, and the accompanying Note by the Director-General (EC-56/DG.6 C-14/DG.3, dated 3 April 2009).  The Conference noted this report. 



 
Subitem 16(d): Transfers of funds between programmes in 2008 
16.5
 In accordance with Regulation 4.5 of the OPCW Financial Regulations and Rules, all transfers between Budget programmes shall be reported to the Conference.  The Conference noted the Note by the Director-General on this matter (EC-55/DG.7 C-14/DG.2, dated 2 February 2009). 



 
Subitem 16(e): Adoption of the International Public Sector Accounting Standards 
 
16.6
 The Conference considered and adopted a decision on the adoption of the International Public Sector Accounting Standards (C-14/DEC.5, dated 2 December 2009). 



 
17. AGENDA ITEM SEVENTEEN  Fostering of international cooperation for peaceful purposes in the field of chemical activities 
17.1
 At its Thirteenth Session, the Conference requested the Council to continue to work, pursuant to previous decisions, to intensify consultations at regular intervals to develop concrete measures within an agreed framework to ensure the full implementation of Article XI, and to report back to the Conference at its Fourteenth Session (paragraph 17.3 of C-13/5). 



 
17.2
 The Conference also adopted a decision on the matter (C-14/DEC.11, dated 4 December 2009). 



   
18. AGENDA ITEM EIGHTEEN  Ensuring the universality of the Convention 
 
18.1 The Conference noted the annual report by the Director-General on the implementation of the action plan for the universality of the Convention during the period from 19 November 2008 to 11 September 2009 (EC-58/DG.9 C-14/DG.8, dated 29 September 2009). 
18.2 The Conference considered and adopted a decision on the universality of the Convention and the further implementation of the universality action plan (C-14/DEC.7, dated 2 December 2009). 
19. AGENDA ITEM NINETEEN  The OPCW Programme for Africa 
The Conference noted the Note by the Director-General on the Programme to Strengthen Cooperation with Africa (C-14/DG.12, dated 18 November 2009). 
20. AGENDA ITEM TWENTY  Appointment of the Director-General
